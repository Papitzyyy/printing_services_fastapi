Papiprints: A Printing Services System
Github Repository Link: https://github.com/Papitzyyy/printing_services_fastapi

1. Project Overvlew 
Papiprints is a backend web application that uses FastAPI to control student printing services. The system offers a high performance API for submitting print orders, keeping order history for administrators and students to easily access, and computing charges depending on document properties.

2. Problem Statement
The current printing process is a major headache for everyone on campus. Students waste time in long lines, and staff struggle to keep up with manual tasks, leading to a stressful and disorganized environment.
	Pricing Confusion: Manual calculations often lead to different prices for the same causing 	frustration.
	The "Waiting Game": Without a digital queue, students are forced to stand in line just to submit 	a file.
	Lost Tracks: There is no digital paper trail, making it impossible for students to see what they’ve    	spent or for staff to manage orders.
	Workload Stress: Handling dozens of physical requests at once without a system makes it easy 	for jobs to get lost or delayed.

3. Objectives
The main goal of Papiprints is to make campus printing as fast and painless as possible through a smart, digital backend.
	Automate Pricing: Calculate costs instantly so there’s no guesswork or overcharging.
	Reduce Wait Times: Allow students to submit print jobs digitally to cut down on physical 	queues.
	Digital Records: Keep a clear, searchable history of every order for both students and staff.
	Simplify Workflow: Organize all incoming print requests into a single, manageable digital list.

4. Users
Since Papiprints is a FastAPI-driven system, it serves two distinct groups with different needs.
	Students: The primary customers who upload document details, select print options, and track 	their order costs.
	Printing Staff/Admins: The operators who manage the incoming digital queue, update order 	statuses, and verify payments.
